\section{Mechanical test setup}\label{sec:Mechanical_testsetup.tex}
The mechanical test setup available at Aalborg University can be seen on \figref{fig:Mec_abcd}. It includes four of the motors described in \secref{Maxon_Motor} attached to the four rotational plates seen on \figref{fig:Mec_a} and \figref{fig:Mec_b}. These plates connects the motors and the Endowrist, such that the Endowrist can be manipulated. See \figref{fig:Mec_c} and \figref{fig:Mec_d} for the connection of the Endowrist to the Endowrist holder.
